🌾 Best Minecraft Farms

🔥 Must-Have Farms

  • Iron Farm → infinite iron for gear
  • XP Farm → enchant faster
  • Creeper Farm → gunpowder for rockets
  • Sugarcane Farm → books + trading
  • Food Farm → automatic wheat / carrot farms

⚡ Pro Tip

Build farms near your base + use chunk loaders (or stay close) so they keep working. More farms = easier survival + faster PvP gear.
