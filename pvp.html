⚔️ PvP Mods to Improve Your Skills (Fabric)

🎯 Combat Helper Mods

  • AppleSkin — shows better hunger bar for PvP
  • Armour HUD — shows armor durability on screen
  • BactroMod — fog changes, fire HUD, shield offset
  • HealthIndicators — shows exact enemy health
  • InventoryHUD — see inventory items without opening it
  • ShieldStatus — shows when shield is used or broken
  • CrosshairAddons — improves crosshair for aiming and precision

🏆 Advanced PvP Tracking

  • TotemCounter + Ukulib — shows how many totems a player has popped directly on their nametag.
  • EffectTimerPlus — shows the time remaining for potion effects
  • TipTapShow — displays a small screen at the top-left showing CPS and movement
